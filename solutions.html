On-Premise

We provide a development, execution, and management environment for desktop robots for customers who want to manage and operate robots deployed in each department.

Recommended if:

  • Robot development and maintenance personnel available in-house. (VBA skills).
  • Robot development and maintenance needs to be done in-house due to the involvement with the core systems or compliance requirements.

Managed by Heartcore

We create the robots and manage the robots for you. The robots are executed in your environment with Heartcore providing remote management, monitoring and technical support.

Recommended if:

  • No robot development and maintenance personnel available in-house.
  • You want to introduce RPA with a simple robot (small number of steps) and want to minimize the setup work for now.
  • You want to create and manage robots with our expertise.
