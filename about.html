The company

Heatcore, Inc., is established in Japan by Kanno Sumitaka in 2009. The company started out with helping companies effectively managing content with its powerful CMS (content management system). Since then, the company has expanded offerings to help companies managing business processes. Clients include leading Japanese enterprises such as Japan Airlines and Hitachi.

Heartcore technology US is established in 2019 with a dedicated focus on RPA (Robotic Process Automation) technology.
